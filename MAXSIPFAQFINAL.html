“We are a service provider for the Federal Government’s Affordable Connectivity Program, provides all eligible individuals a Tablet with FREE monthly internet.”

ACP – stands for Affordable Connectivity Program that is part of a Federal Communications Commission benefit given by US Government that help and ensure families and households struggling to afford internet service. This new benefit will connect eligible families and households to jobs, school, critical healthcare services, virtual classrooms and so much more online. The Affordable Connectivity Program is limited to one device per tax household.
